Active meetup

During this project me and my team were supposed to design an app to match people with similar training interests. The first step was to determine who our main user was and to create a persona. This was done by conducting interviews with a total of 12 people of various age and gender. We also carried out an ethnographic study on Facebook groups and running communities online.
